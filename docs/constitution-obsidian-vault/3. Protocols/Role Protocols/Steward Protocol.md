This protocol defines the responsibilities, expectations, and processes for Stewards of the Network Nations Alliance.

## Instantiation

**Trigger Type:** Status-based

**Trigger Condition:**
- An individual holds the Steward role

## Authority & Oversight

| Role | Authority | Accountability |
|------|-----------|----------------|
| [[2. Structure/Roles/Steward|Steward]] | Operates per this protocol | Steward Council, Alliance Assembly |
| [[Steward Council]] | Collective stewardship decisions | Alliance Assembly |
| [[Alliance Assembly]] | Ultimate oversight of Stewards | The Constitution |

## Steward Selection

### Current

Stewards are self-appointed with consent of existing Stewards:
1. Individual expresses interest in becoming Steward
2. Existing Stewards discuss and reach consent
3. If consent reached, individual joins Steward Council
4. Alliance Assembly informed at next gathering

### Future

Selection process to be determined by Alliance Assembly. This protocol will be amended to reflect the decided process.

## Core Responsibilities

**Infrastructure Stewardship**
- Maintain Alliance platforms and tools
- Ensure systems remain functional and accessible
- Coordinate technical support and improvements

**Asset Management**
- Protect and evolve shared assets
- Maintain treasury per [[3. Protocols/Asset Protocols/Treasury Management Protocol|Treasury Management Protocol]]
- Ensure constitutional integrity

**Facilitation**
- Enable Alliance Assembly gatherings
- Support activity formation and coordination
- Coordinate Steward Council operations

**Communication**
- Maintain Alliance communication channels
- Represent Alliance in external contexts
- Keep Members informed of relevant developments

**Operational Coordination**
- Respond to Member needs and questions
- Coordinate among Stewards on responsibilities
- Ensure continuity of Alliance functions

## Rights & Responsibilities

### Rights

| Category | Right |
|----------|-------|
| **Governance** | Full Member rights plus Steward Council participation |
| **Operations** | Administrative access to all Alliance platforms |
| **Treasury** | Signatory on multi-sig wallet |
| **Representation** | Speak for Alliance in appropriate contexts |

### Responsibilities

| Category | Responsibility |
|----------|----------------|
| **Availability** | Responsive to operational needs within reasonable time |
| **Participation** | Active engagement in Steward Council |
| **Stewardship** | Careful management of Alliance assets and resources |
| **Service** | Orientation toward serving membership, not directing it |

## Expectations

**Active Participation:** Participate in weekly/bi-weekly Steward Council coordination
**Responsiveness:** Respond to operational matters within 48 hours when possible
**Collaboration:** Work constructively with fellow Stewards
**Transparency:** Document decisions and share information with Members

## Transition

**Stepping Down:**
- Steward provides 30 days notice when possible
- Steward supports knowledge transfer to remaining Stewards
- Responsibilities reassigned within Steward Council

**Removal:**
- Process to be determined by Alliance Assembly
- Until then, Steward Council addresses concerns internally

## Annual Review

Steward Council conducts annual self-assessment:
- Individual reflection on contributions and challenges
- Peer feedback within Council
- Collective assessment of Council function
- Adjustments to responsibilities or practices as needed

## Completion & Outputs

**Protocol is ongoing while:**
- Individual holds Steward role

**Outputs:**
- Operational records
- Decision documentation
- Annual review notes
