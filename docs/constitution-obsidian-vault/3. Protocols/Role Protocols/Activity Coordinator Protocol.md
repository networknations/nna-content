This protocol defines the responsibilities, expectations, and processes for Activity Coordinators within the Network Nations Alliance.

## Instantiation

**Trigger Type:** Status-based

**Trigger Condition:**
- An individual holds the [[Activity Coordinator]] role for an active activity

## Authority & Oversight

| Role | Authority | Accountability |
|------|-----------|----------------|
| [[Activity Coordinators]] | Coordinates their activity | Steward Council, Activity participants |
| [[Steward Council]] | Supports activities, provides resources | Alliance Assembly |

## Activity Coordinators Emergence

Activity Coordinators emerge through the [[Activity Protocol]]:
1. Member proposes activity aligned with Alliance purpose
2. Two other Members express support
3. Activity is announced to Alliance
4. Proposer becomes Activity Coordinator

## Core Responsibilities

**Coordination**
- Organize activity participants and working sessions
- Maintain momentum and track progress
- Facilitate communication among participants
- Make operational decisions for the activity

**Communication**
- Keep Alliance informed of activity status
- Share updates at Alliance Assembly gatherings
- Document learnings and outputs
- Respond to inquiries about the activity

**Alignment**
- Ensure activity remains aligned with Alliance purpose
- Uphold Alliance values within activity spaces
- Escalate concerns to Steward Council when needed

**Completion**
- Bring activity to clear conclusion or transition
- Document outcomes and lessons learned
- Hand off or archive as appropriate

## Rights & Responsibilities

### Rights

| Category | Right |
|----------|-------|
| **Authority** | Coordinate activity operations and decisions |
| **Resources** | Request Alliance support through Steward Council |
| **Visibility** | Activity featured on Alliance platforms |
| **Autonomy** | Self-organize within Alliance alignment |

### Responsibilities

| Category | Responsibility |
|----------|----------------|
| **Communication** | Regular updates to Alliance |
| **Alignment** | Keep activity aligned with Alliance purpose |
| **Ethics** | Uphold Alliance values in activity spaces |
| **Completion** | Conclude or transition activity appropriately |

## Check-ins with Steward Council

Activity Coordinators connect with Steward Council:
- **Quarterly:** Brief status update (async or at gathering)
- **As needed:** Request resources or support
- **At conclusion:** Final report on outcomes

## Resource Requests

Activity Coordinators may request Alliance resources:
- Platform access or tools
- Communication support (announcements, etc.)
- Financial support (per [[3. Protocols/Asset Protocols/Treasury Management Protocol|Treasury Management Protocol]])
- Connection to relevant Members or external parties

Requests are made to Steward Council and decided per [[Steward Council Protocol]].

## Transition

**Handing Off Leadership:**
- Activity Coordinator identifies and orients successor
- Notifies Steward Council of transition
- New Activity Coordinator continues per this protocol

**Concluding Activity:**
- Activity Coordinator determines activity has reached natural conclusion
- Documents outcomes and lessons learned
- Notifies Alliance and archives materials

**Activity Becoming Inactive:**
- If activity becomes dormant, Steward Council may check in
- Activity Coordinator may conclude, pause, or revitalize
- Inactive activities may be archived after 6 months without activity

## Completion & Outputs

**Protocol is ongoing while:**
- Individual holds Activity Coordinator role for active activity

**Outputs:**
- Activity updates and records
- Final report at activity conclusion
- Archived materials
