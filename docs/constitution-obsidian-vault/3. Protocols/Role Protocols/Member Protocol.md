This protocol defines the expectations, rights, and participation ethics for Members of the Network Nations Alliance.

## Instantiation

**Trigger Type:** Status-based

**Trigger Condition:**
- An individual or organization holds the Member role

## Authority & Oversight

| Role | Authority | Accountability |
|------|-----------|----------------|
| [[Member]] | Participates per this protocol | Alliance Assembly |
| [[Steward Council]] | Supports Member participation | Alliance Assembly |

## Participation Ethics

All Members commit to the following participation ethics:

**Good Faith Engagement**
- Participate with genuine intent to contribute
- Assume good faith in others unless shown otherwise
- Engage constructively even in disagreement

**Honest Communication**
- Represent yourself and your work accurately
- Share relevant information that affects collective decisions
- Acknowledge uncertainty and limitations

**Respect for Others**
- Honor the dignity of all participants
- Listen actively and make space for diverse voices
- Avoid personal attacks and dismissive behavior

**Accountability**
- Follow through on commitments
- Accept feedback and reflect on impact
- Acknowledge mistakes and work to repair harm

**Alignment**
- Act in accordance with Alliance purpose and values
- Raise concerns through appropriate channels
- Support decisions once made, even if you disagreed

## Rights & Responsibilities

### Rights

| Category | Right |
|----------|-------|
| **Governance** | Voice in Alliance Assembly, proposal rights, voting participation |
| **Community** | Access to Telegram channels, gatherings, activities |
| **Resources** | Access to Network Nations Index, Member Directory, Notion workspace |
| **Information** | Receive updates, financial reports, decision records |

### Responsibilities

| Category | Responsibility |
|----------|----------------|
| **Participation** | Engage in Alliance Assembly at least annually |
| **Ethics** | Uphold participation ethics in all Alliance spaces |
| **Community** | Support fellow members, contribute to healthy culture |
| **Communication** | Keep contact information current, respond to important matters |

## Member Types

The Alliance welcomes both individual and organizational Members:

**Individual Members:** Natural persons participating in their own capacity

**Organizational Members:** Organizations (including Network Nations) participating through designated representatives
- Organizations designate a primary contact
- Organizations may change representatives with notice to Stewards
- Representatives participate as themselves while also representing organizational perspective

## Engagement Expectations

To remain in good standing, Members are expected to:
- Participate in at least one Alliance Assembly gathering per year
- Maintain current contact information
- Respond to direct inquiries within reasonable time
- Not be in unresolved conflict under [[3. Protocols/Cultural Protocols/Conflict Transformation Protocol|Conflict Transformation Protocol]]

Members not meeting these expectations may be contacted for re-engagement or graceful transition out.
