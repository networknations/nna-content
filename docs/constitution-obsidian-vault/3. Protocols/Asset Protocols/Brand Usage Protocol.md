This protocol governs how Network Nations Alliance brand assets may be used — protecting brand integrity while enabling appropriate use by members and partners.

## Instantiation

**Trigger Type:** Action-based

**Trigger Condition:**
- A Member or external party wishes to use Alliance brand assets
- Brand misuse is identified

## Authority & Oversight

| Role | Authority | Accountability |
|------|-----------|----------------|
| [[Steward Council]] | Approves non-standard usage, addresses misuse | Alliance Assembly |
| [[Member]]s | May use within guidelines without approval | Steward Council |

## Brand Assets Covered

- Alliance name: "Network Nations Alliance"
- Logo and visual identity
- Taglines and official descriptions
- Domain names and social handles

## Usage Guidelines

### Permitted Without Approval

Members in good standing may:
- Reference Alliance membership ("Member of Network Nations Alliance")
- Use logo on personal sites/materials indicating membership
- Share Alliance content with attribution
- Describe Alliance activities accurately

### Requires Approval

- Using Alliance brand in commercial contexts
- Creating merchandise with Alliance branding
- Using brand in ways that imply official endorsement
- Significant modifications to logo or visual identity
- Co-branding arrangements

### Never Permitted

- Implying Alliance endorsement of products, services, or positions without authorization
- Using brand in ways that conflict with Alliance values
- Misrepresenting relationship with Alliance
- Using brand in political campaign materials

## Rights & Responsibilities

### Members

**Rights:**
- Use brand within permitted guidelines
- Request approval for other uses
- Receive response within 14 days

**Responsibilities:**
- Use brand accurately and appropriately
- Follow visual identity guidelines
- Not imply endorsement without approval

### Steward Council

**Rights:**
- Approve or deny usage requests
- Take action on brand misuse
- Update guidelines as needed

**Responsibilities:**
- Respond to requests within 14 days
- Apply guidelines consistently
- Protect brand integrity
- Address misuse promptly

## Process Sequence (Approval Requests)

| Step | Action | Actor | Duration | Output |
|------|--------|-------|----------|--------|
| 1 | Submit request | Requester | — | Request logged |
| 2 | Review | Steward | Within 14 days | Decision made |
| 3 | Communication | Steward | Within 48 hours | Requester notified |

## Addressing Misuse

When brand misuse is identified:
1. Steward contacts party requesting correction
2. If not corrected within 14 days, Steward Council determines further action
3. Actions may include: public clarification, cease and desist, membership review

## Completion & Outputs

**Protocol completes when:**
- Usage request is approved or denied
- Misuse is corrected

**Outputs:**
- Usage approval record
- Misuse resolution record
