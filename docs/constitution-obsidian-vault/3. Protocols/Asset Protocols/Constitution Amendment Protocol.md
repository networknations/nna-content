This protocol governs how the Network Nations Alliance Constitution is amended — ensuring changes receive adequate consideration while remaining adaptable to evolving needs.

## Instantiation

**Trigger Type:** Action-based

**Trigger Condition:**
- A Member submits a formal amendment proposal

## Authority & Oversight

| Role | Authority | Accountability |
|------|-----------|----------------|
| [[Alliance Assembly]] | Approves or rejects amendments | The Constitution |
| [[Steward Council]] | Manages amendment process | Alliance Assembly |
| Proposal Author | Develops and presents amendment | Alliance Assembly |

## Amendment Types

| Type | Description | Threshold |
|------|-------------|-----------|
| **Minor** | Clarifications, typo fixes, formatting | Lazy consensus (14 days) |
| **Standard** | New protocols, role changes, structural adjustments | Supermajority (2/3) |
| **Fundamental** | Changes to Identity, purpose, core values | Supermajority (2/3) + 30-day delay |

## Rights & Responsibilities

### Any Member

**Rights:**
- Propose amendments to the Constitution
- Comment on proposed amendments
- Vote on amendments at Alliance Assembly

**Responsibilities:**
- Propose amendments in good faith
- Consider impact on Alliance purpose and function
- Engage constructively in discussion

### Steward Council

**Rights:**
- Classify amendment type
- Set timeline for consideration
- Recommend approval or rejection

**Responsibilities:**
- Ensure proposal is properly formatted
- Facilitate adequate discussion period
- Maintain amendment records
- Implement approved amendments

## Process Sequence

| Step | Action | Actor | Duration | Output |
|------|--------|-------|----------|--------|
| 1 | Draft proposal | Member | — | Amendment text |
| 2 | Submit proposal | Member | — | Proposal logged |
| 3 | Classification | Steward Council | 48 hours | Type determined |
| 4 | Discussion period | All Members | 14-30 days | Feedback collected |
| 5 | Revision (optional) | Author | As needed | Revised proposal |
| 6 | Vote | Alliance Assembly | At gathering | Decision made |
| 7 | Implementation | Steward Council | Within 7 days | Constitution updated |

### Step 1-2: Proposal Submission

Amendment proposals must include:
- Specific text to be added, removed, or changed
- Rationale for the change
- Impact assessment (what this affects)
- Proposed amendment type

### Step 3: Classification

Steward Council reviews the proposal and confirms or adjusts the amendment type based on scope and impact.

### Step 4: Discussion Period

- **Minor amendments:** 14-day comment period via async channels
- **Standard amendments:** 14-day discussion + Alliance Assembly deliberation
- **Fundamental amendments:** 30-day discussion + Alliance Assembly deliberation

### Step 5: Revision

Author may revise proposal based on feedback. Significant revisions restart the discussion period.

### Step 6: Vote

- **Minor:** Lazy consensus — passes if no objections in 14 days
- **Standard/Fundamental:** Supermajority vote at Alliance Assembly gathering (2/3 of participating Members)

### Step 7: Implementation

Approved amendments are incorporated into the Constitution within 7 days. Version number and date updated.

## Completion & Outputs

**Protocol completes when:**
- Amendment is approved and implemented
- Amendment is rejected
- Author withdraws proposal

**Outputs:**
- Updated Constitution (if approved)
- Amendment record with rationale and vote
- Notification to all Members

## Exceptions & Edge Cases

- **Emergency amendments:** In rare cases requiring immediate action, Steward Council may implement provisional changes pending ratification at next Assembly (requires unanimous Steward consent)
- **Conflicting proposals:** If multiple proposals address same section, they are considered in order received; subsequent proposals may need revision
- **Ratification of provisional changes:** If provisional changes are not ratified, they are reversed
