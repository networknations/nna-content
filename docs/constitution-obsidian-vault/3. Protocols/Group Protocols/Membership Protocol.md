This protocol governs how individuals and organizations join the Network Nations Alliance, maintain membership, and depart.

## Instantiation

**Trigger Type:** Action-based

**Trigger Condition:**
- An individual or organization submits a membership application
- A Member requests departure
- The [[3. Protocols/Cultural Protocols/Conflict Transformation Protocol|Conflict Transformation Protocol]] concludes with a recommendation for membership action

## Authority & Oversight

| Role | Authority | Accountability |
|------|-----------|----------------|
| [[Steward Council]] | Reviews applications, makes membership decisions | Alliance Assembly |
| [[2. Structure/Roles/Steward|Steward]] | Individual review of applications | Steward Council |
| Applicant/Member | Submits application or departure request | Steward Council |

## Membership Criteria

**Alignment:** Demonstrated alignment with Alliance purpose, values, and worldview
**Interest:** Genuine interest in Network Nations concept and movement
**Commitment:** Willingness to participate and uphold participation ethics
**Good Faith:** No known conflicts with Alliance values or existing Members

Both individuals and organizations are welcome. Organizations participate through designated representatives.

## Rights & Responsibilities

### Steward Council

**Rights:**
- Review and decide on membership applications
- Request additional information from applicants
- Determine membership status following [[3. Protocols/Cultural Protocols/Conflict Transformation Protocol|Conflict Transformation Protocol]]

**Responsibilities:**
- Review applications within 30 days
- Apply criteria consistently and fairly
- Document decisions with rationale
- Communicate decisions to applicants

### Applicant

**Rights:**
- Apply for membership
- Receive decision within 30 days
- Receive explanation if application is declined
- Reapply after 3 months if declined

**Responsibilities:**
- Provide accurate information
- Respond to requests for clarification
- Affirm alignment with Constitution upon acceptance

## Process Sequence (Joining)

| Step | Action             | Actor                | Duration             | Output                               |
| ---- | ------------------ | -------------------- | -------------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| 1    | Submit application | Applicant            | —                    | Application received                 |
| 2    | Review             | Steward Council      | Within 30 days       | Decision made                        |
| 3    | Notification       | Steward              | Within 48 hours      | Applicant informed                   |
| 4    | Onboarding         | New Member + Steward | Following acceptance | Access granted, orientation provided |

### Step 1: Application

Applicants submit membership application including:
- Name (individual or organization)
- Brief background and interest in Network Nations
- Statement of alignment with Alliance purpose and values
- Contact information

### Step 2: Review

Steward Council reviews application using lazy consensus:
- If no concerns raised within 30 days, application approved
- If concern raised, Council discusses and decides
- Majority of Council required to decline application

### Step 3: Decision Communication

- **Approved:** Applicant welcomed and directed to onboarding
- **Declined:** Applicant notified with explanation; may reapply after 3 months

### Step 4: Onboarding

New Members receive:
- Access to Alliance platforms (Telegram, Notion)
- Orientation to Constitution and key protocols
- Introduction to community
- Invitation to upcoming Alliance Assembly

## Maintaining Membership

Members maintain good standing by:
- Participating in at least one Alliance Assembly annually
- Upholding participation ethics
- Keeping contact information current
- Not being in unresolved conflict

## Voluntary Departure

Members may depart at any time:
1. Member notifies any Steward of departure
2. Access concluded within 7 days
3. Member removed from directory

No waiting period or approval required.

## Involuntary Removal

Following [[3. Protocols/Cultural Protocols/Conflict Transformation Protocol|Conflict Transformation Protocol]] recommendation:
1. Steward Council reviews recommendation
2. Council decides by majority vote
3. If removal confirmed, Member notified with explanation
4. Access concluded within 7 days

## Re-engagement

Members who have become inactive may be contacted by Stewards:
- Inquiry about continued interest
- Invitation to re-engage
- If no response after 30 days, may be moved to inactive status
- Inactive members may reactivate by contacting Stewards

## Completion & Outputs

**Protocol completes when:**
- Application is decided and (if approved) onboarding complete
- Departure is processed

**Outputs:**
- Membership directory updated
- Decision record maintained
- Communication to applicant/member

## Exceptions & Edge Cases

- **Founding members:** Initial members may be grandfathered without formal application
- **Urgent safety concerns:** Steward Council may take immediate protective action pending full process
- **Organizational representative change:** Organizations notify Stewards; no new application required
