The Network Nations Alliance fulfills its purpose through five core functions:

**Convening**
Bringing together individuals and organizations exploring Network Nations through gatherings, assemblies, and collaborative spaces — both online and in-person.

**Knowledge Stewardship**
Cultivating, curating, and sharing knowledge about Network Nations — including definitions, frameworks, case studies, and emerging practices.

**Publishing**
Creating and distributing content that advances understanding of Network Nations — including research, guides, analyses, and commentary.

**Activity Support**
Enabling self-organized activities that advance the movement — including mapping projects, alliance-building, initiatives, grants, courses, and other emergent efforts.
