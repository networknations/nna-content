The Alliance commits to the following mandates:

**Definition Stewardship**
Maintain and evolve the Network Nation definition with care. Balance conceptual coherence with room for diverse interpretations. Prevent dilution or co-option of the core concept while remaining open to its evolution.

**Open Curation**
Keep the Index open and accessible. Moderate for alignment, not quality judgments. Err toward inclusion. Never position ourselves as gatekeepers of who "counts" as a Network Nation.

**Narrative Care**
Engage thoughtfully with how Network Nations are represented in public discourse. Practice mimetic stewardship rather than memetic warfare. Maintain cultural appeal beyond academic circles.

**Movement Over Organization**
Serve the broader Network Nations movement, not our own institutional growth. Support initiatives we don't control. Celebrate successes that happen outside Alliance spaces.

---

## What We Are Not

To maintain focus and integrity, the Alliance explicitly is **not**:

- A Network Nation itself (we are a coordinative group)
- A hierarchical organization controlling member projects
- A certification authority or gatekeeper
- A regulatory body imposing requirements on Network Nations
- A commercial entity or platform company
