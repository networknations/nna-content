# Freedom to Participate

You are free to take any action that fulfills the [[1. Identity/Purpose|Purpose]] of the Alliance and the [[2. Structure/Roles/Roles Index|Roles Index]] that you hold — as long as you do not violate any [[3. Protocols/Protocols Index|Protocols Index]]; and you seek to uphold the [[1. Identity/Values|Values]] and intent of this Constitution.
