**Author:** [[people/austin-wade-smith|Austin Wade Smith]] / Regen Foundation

This series explores salient challenges and opportunities in regenerative economics broadly, and on the blockchain specifically. The goal is to bring capacity to a diverse public to be critically informed about the intersection of climate justice, social justice, and web3 — seen as an essential prerequisite for communities to own and govern the revolutionary infrastructures which support a systemic transition in our definitions of value away from extraction and towards regeneration.

Directly relevant to [[concepts/commons|commons]], [[concepts/mutualization|mutualization]], and the regenerative economics strand of [[concepts/network-nations|Network Nations]].
