**Author:** Christopher F. Roth (2015)
**Published:** Litwin Books

A comprehensive guide to separatist movements and aspirant nations, from Abkhazia to Zanzibar. At 634 pages, this reference work catalogues the full landscape of groups seeking self-determination and statehood outside the current international order — providing important context for understanding the historical precedents and parallels to [[concepts/network-nations|Network Nations]].
