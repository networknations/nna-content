This book proposes a new solution to the problem of the migration crisis engulfing the world by creating safe harbours, or Free Global Cities. Relevant to discussions of [[concepts/functional-sovereignty|functional sovereignty]] and alternative governance models that operate outside traditional nation-state frameworks.
