Explores how charter cities — new cities with special governance rules — could transform the developing world. Related to [[people/paul-romer|Paul Romer]]'s charter cities concept and relevant as a parallel model to [[concepts/network-nations|Network Nations]], though with a more territorial and top-down approach.
