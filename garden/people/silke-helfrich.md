Commons scholar and co-founder of the Commons Strategies Group alongside [[people/david-bollier|David Bollier]] and [[people/michel-bauwens|Michel Bauwens]]. Co-author with Bollier of *Free, Fair and Alive: The Insurgent Power of the Commons* (2019) and *The Wealth of the Commons* (2012). Her work bridges commons theory with practical governance design, emphasizing commoning as a set of social practices and relationships rather than a static resource category.
