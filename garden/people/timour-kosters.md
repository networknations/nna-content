Practitioner and thinker in the network nations design space, appearing alongside [[people/jon-hillis|Jon Hillis]] to explore the concept of entanglement — the design of voluntary interdependencies between communities. His work addresses the practical dimensions of how communities build durable bonds through shared capital, shared identity systems, and mutual mobility across nodes.
