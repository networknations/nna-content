Co-founder of [[organizations/opencivics|OpenCivics]] and a participatory futurist, designing sociotechnical systems and immersive experiences that empower communities to co-create regenerative futures. Organizer and movement builder, and a core member of the Network Nations Alliance team. Appears in the final episode of the Network Nations podcast series alongside [[people/benjamin-life|Benjamin Life]], discussing how to catalyze the Network Nations concept into a real, durable movement. Her work focuses on bridging the conceptual framework of Network Nations with the practical work of coalition building across existing communities, organizations, and practitioners in the space.
