Founder of Gitcoin, one of the largest public goods funding platforms in the Web3 ecosystem. A key figure in the regenerative finance (ReFi) and digital public goods movements, Owocki has pioneered mechanisms like quadratic funding to direct collective resources toward open-source and community-driven projects. He is also involved in GreenPill Network, which is the series context for the Network Nations podcast.

His work embodies the Network Nations principle of [[concepts/stake-based-governance|stake-based governance]] and [[concepts/commons|commons]]-based resource management — using cryptographic infrastructure to fund shared goods beyond state and market mechanisms. His perspectives on collective identity within Web3 communities and the cultural fabric of decentralized ecosystems contribute to the episode on network nation identity.
