Designer and researcher at Elicit, known for her work on local-first software and "barefoot programming." Her talk "Home-cooked Software and Barefoot Programmers" at the Local-First Conference argues that machine learning and language models are about to create a golden age of local software, home-cooked apps, and barefoot developers — enabling communities to build their own tools.

Relevant to the [[concepts/network-nations|Network Nations]] interest in [[concepts/networked-technologies|networked technologies]] that empower communities to build and maintain their own infrastructure rather than depending on centralized platforms.
