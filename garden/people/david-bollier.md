Commons scholar, activist, and co-founder of the Commons Strategies Group (alongside [[people/silke-helfrich|Silke Helfrich]] and [[people/michel-bauwens|Michel Bauwens]]). Author of numerous books on commons-based governance including *Think Like a Commoner* (2014) and *Free, Fair and Alive: The Insurgent Power of the Commons* (co-authored with Helfrich, 2019). Editor of the Commoner blog.

Bollier's work provides foundational theoretical vocabulary for the [[concepts/commons|commons]] principle within Network Nations. His articulation of commoning as an active social practice — not merely a resource pool but a mode of collective governance and relationship — underpins the Network Nations distinction between commons-based stewardship and both state bureaucracy and market extraction.
