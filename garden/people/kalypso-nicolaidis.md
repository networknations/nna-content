Political scientist whose work on European governance and deliberative democracy is relevant to the Network Nations project. Her talk "Beyond the Conference on the Future of Europe: A Permanent Deliberative Assembly" explores how participatory and deliberative processes can reshape governance at scale.

Her vision of permanent deliberative structures resonates with the [[concepts/network-nations|Network Nations]] emphasis on [[concepts/self-governance|self-governance]] and [[concepts/collective-action|collective action]] through ongoing participatory processes rather than one-off consultations.
