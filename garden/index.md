Welcome to the Network Nations Knowledge Garden — a living, interlinked knowledge base exploring the theory and practice of network nations.

Network nations are globally distributed communities of choice that build and govern their own digital civic infrastructure to cultivate trust at scale. The Network Nations Alliance is a cooperative alliance of builders, researchers, organizations, and activists reimagining digital networks as tools to empower civil society beyond states and markets.

This knowledge garden collects the concepts, governance frameworks, reading materials, and organizational landscape that define this emerging design space.

## How to Navigate This Garden

This site is a **digital garden** — a style of knowledge base where ideas are cultivated over time rather than published as finished articles. Pages are interconnected freely using **wikilinks**, so you can follow threads of thought from one concept to the next.

Here are a few ways to explore:

- **Follow links** — each page links to related concepts, governance structures, and readings. Let curiosity guide you.
- **Use the graph view** — see how ideas connect to each other visually. The graph reveals clusters and relationships that aren't obvious from reading alone.
- **Search** — if you're looking for something specific, use the search bar to jump directly to a topic.

Pages link to each other freely. There is no single "correct" reading order — every path through the garden is valid.
