**Location:** Near Stockholm, Sweden
**Dates:** 14–20 July 2025
**Organized by:** [[people/primavera-de-filippi|Primavera de Filippi]], [[people/felix-beer|Felix Beer]], and collaborators

A one-week transdisciplinary workshop at Emerge Lakefront to co-develop the theory and practice of Network Nations — a framework for understanding how communities can organize, govern, and build shared identity through digital networks.
