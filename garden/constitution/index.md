Welcome to the **Network Nations Alliance** -- a coordinative assembly dedicated to exploring, defining, and stewarding the emerging concept of Network Nations. We are a movement and community of practice fostering distributed experimentation and collaborative learning around new forms of social organization.

**We are not a Network Nation ourselves**, but rather the gardeners tending the conceptual commons from which diverse Network Nations can emerge and flourish.

This document is the governing agreement for all members of the Network Nations Alliance. It encodes our collective identity, organizational structure, and the protocols by which we coordinate together.

By joining the Alliance, you affirm alignment with the agreements associated with your role and commit to the participation ethics outlined herein.

## Freedom to Participate

You are free to take any action that fulfills the [[constitution/identity/purpose|Purpose]] of the Alliance and the roles that you hold -- as long as you do not violate any [[constitution/protocols/decision-making|Protocols]]; and you seek to uphold the [[constitution/identity/values|Values]] and intent of this Constitution.
