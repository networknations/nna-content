We connect and support individuals and organizations exploring Network Nations -- enabling collective sense-making, coordination, and experimentation around new forms of self-sovereign networks.

vs

Cultivate a vibrant community of practice that: establishes Network Nations as a well-defined standard for community organization; launches new Network Nation activities from our cohort; creates practical governance toolkits; amplifies research and writing; strategically stewards the public narrative; and maintains the infrastructure for distributed experimentation and collaborative learning.
