# Freedom to Participate

You are free to take any action that fulfills the [[constitution/identity/purpose|Purpose]] of the Alliance and the roles that you hold -- as long as you do not violate any protocols; and you seek to uphold the [[constitution/identity/values|Values]] and intent of this Constitution.

# Overview of Activity Coordinator Role

See: [[constitution/structure/roles/activity-coordinator|Activity Coordinator]]

# Overview of Activities

Activities are self-organized efforts that advance Alliance purpose, including mapping projects, alliances, initiatives, grants, gatherings, courses, and other emergent efforts.

See [[constitution/protocols/activity|Activity Protocol]] for complete details on activity formation, operation, and conclusion.
