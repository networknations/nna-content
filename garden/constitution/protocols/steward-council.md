This protocol governs how the Steward Council operates -- the coordination, decision-making, and accountability practices for Alliance stewardship.

## Instantiation

**Trigger Type:** Ongoing

**Trigger Condition:**
- The Steward Council exists with one or more Stewards

## Authority & Oversight

| Role | Authority | Accountability |
|------|-----------|----------------|
| [[constitution/structure/groups/steward-council|Steward Council]] | Operational decisions, asset management | Alliance Assembly |
| Individual [[constitution/structure/roles/steward|Steward]]s | Contribute to collective decisions | Steward Council |
| [[constitution/structure/groups/alliance-assembly|Alliance Assembly]] | Ultimate oversight, strategic direction | The Constitution |

## Council Composition

All Stewards are members of the Steward Council. There is no fixed size; the Council expands and contracts based on capacity and need.

## Decision-Making

| Decision Type | Method | Threshold |
|--------------|--------|-----------|
| Operational matters | Lazy consensus | No objections in 48 hours |
| Resource allocation | Consent-based | No paramount objections |
| Policy recommendations | Consent-based | No paramount objections |
| Emergency actions | Any Steward | Ratification within 7 days |

See [[constitution/protocols/decision-making|Decision-Making Protocol]] for method details.

## Meetings

**Regular Coordination**
- Frequency: Weekly or bi-weekly (Council determines)
- Format: Synchronous video call
- Purpose: Operational coordination, decision-making, mutual support

**Async Coordination**
- Ongoing via designated channel
- Used for: Quick decisions, updates, questions between meetings

**Strategic Planning**
- Frequency: Quarterly
- Purpose: Review priorities, assess capacity, plan ahead

## Rights & Responsibilities

### Steward Council (Collective)

**Rights:**
- Make operational decisions for the Alliance
- Manage Alliance assets and resources
- Recommend policy to Alliance Assembly
- Coordinate external representation

**Responsibilities:**
- Maintain Alliance infrastructure and operations
- Support Member participation and activities
- Provide transparency to Alliance Assembly
- Steward resources responsibly

### Individual Stewards

**Rights:**
- Participate in all Council decisions
- Propose agenda items and decisions
- Take emergency action when necessary
- Access all Alliance operational information

**Responsibilities:**
- Attend regular coordination meetings
- Respond to operational matters promptly
- Support fellow Stewards
- Document decisions and actions

## Coordination Practices

**Agenda Setting:** Any Steward may add items; agenda shared before meetings
**Facilitation:** Rotates among Stewards or designated facilitator
**Note-Taking:** Decisions and action items documented
**Action Tracking:** Clear ownership and follow-up on commitments

## Accountability to Alliance Assembly

**Regular Reporting:**
- Updates at each Alliance Assembly gathering
- Quarterly written summaries shared with Members
- Annual comprehensive report

**Transparency:**
- Decision records available to Members on request
- Financial reports per [[constitution/protocols/treasury-management|Treasury Management Protocol]]
- Open to questions and feedback

**Direction-Taking:**
- Alliance Assembly may direct Council on specific matters
- Constitutional changes bind Council operations
- Assembly may override Council decisions (rarely exercised)

## Adding and Removing Stewards

**Adding (through 2027):**
- Candidate expresses interest
- Existing Stewards discuss and reach consent
- If consent, candidate joins Council
- Assembly notified at next gathering

**Removing:**
- Steward may step down voluntarily (see [[constitution/protocols/steward|Steward Protocol]])
- Removal for cause handled through [[constitution/protocols/conflict-transformation|Conflict Transformation Protocol]]
- Post-2027 process to be determined by Assembly

## Completion & Outputs

**Protocol is ongoing while:**
- Steward Council exists

**Outputs:**
- Meeting notes and decision records
- Action item tracking
- Reports to Alliance Assembly

## Exceptions & Edge Cases

- **Single Steward:** If only one Steward remains, they operate with enhanced Assembly oversight until additional Stewards join
- **Steward conflicts:** Stewards recuse from decisions where personally conflicted
- **Capacity constraints:** Council may pause non-essential activities during low-capacity periods
