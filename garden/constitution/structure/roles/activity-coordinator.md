Coordinators of self-organized activities that advance the Network Nations Alliance's purpose.

## Purpose

Activity Coordinators emerge to coordinate specific activities -- mapping projects, alliances, initiatives, grants, gatherings, courses, and other self-organized efforts. They hold responsibility for their activity's coordination while remaining accountable to the Alliance.

## Criteria

- Active Member in good standing
- Clear proposal for activity aligned with Alliance purpose
- Capacity to coordinate the activity
- Support from at least two other Members

## Selection

Activity Coordinators emerge through the [[constitution/protocols/activity|Activity Protocol]]:
1. Member proposes activity with clear scope and purpose
2. Two other Members express support
3. Activity is announced to Alliance Assembly
4. Proposer becomes Activity Coordinator upon activity formation

## Responsibilities

**Coordination:** Organize activity participants, maintain momentum, track progress
**Communication:** Keep Alliance informed of activity status, share learnings and outputs
**Alignment:** Ensure activity remains aligned with Alliance purpose and values
**Completion:** Bring activity to clear conclusion or transition when appropriate

## Benefits

| Category | Access |
|----------|--------|
| **Governance** | All Member rights plus authority over activity coordination |
| **Resources** | May request Alliance resources or support through Steward Council |
| **Visibility** | Activity featured on Alliance platforms and communications |

## Term and Accountability

- **Term:** Duration of the activity
- **Review:** Periodic check-ins with Steward Council
- **Expectations:** Maintain activity communication, uphold Alliance values
- **Transition:** Activity Coordinator may hand off coordination or conclude activity per [[constitution/protocols/activity|Activity Protocol]]
