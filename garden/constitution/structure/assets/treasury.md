## Description

The Network Nations Alliance Treasury holds shared financial resources -- donations, grants, and other funds supporting Alliance operations and activities. It is secured through a multi-signature wallet requiring multiple Steward approvals for transactions.

## Custodian

[[constitution/structure/groups/steward-council|Steward Council]]

## Signatories

All Stewards are signatories on the multi-sig wallet.

## Access

- **View balance:** Members
- **Propose transactions:** Stewards
- **Approve transactions:** Multiple Stewards per wallet threshold

## Transaction Thresholds

| Amount | Required Signers |
|--------|-----------------|
| Under $500 | 2 of N Stewards |
| $500 - $2,000 | 3 of N Stewards |
| Over $2,000 | Majority of Stewards |
