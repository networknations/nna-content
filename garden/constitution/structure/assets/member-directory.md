## Description

The Member Directory maintains current information about all Alliance members -- individuals and organizations. It is a private resource accessible only to members, supporting connection and coordination within the Alliance.

## Custodian

[[constitution/structure/groups/steward-council|Steward Council]]

## Access

- **View:** Public
- **Update own entry:** Members
- **Administer:** Stewards

## Contents

- Member name (individual or organization)
- Contact information
- Role(s) held
- Activities involved in
- Join date

## Privacy

Member information is not shared externally without consent. Members may limit visibility of specific contact details within the directory.
