A framework and community of practice for building belonging and mutual aid at the small-group scale. Microsolidarity proposes that large-scale social change begins with small, high-trust groups (crews, pods) that can federate and scale through network structures.

Relevant to [[concepts/network-nations|Network Nations]] as a practical methodology for the human-scale building blocks of networked governance — echoing the [[concepts/interdependence|interdependence]] and [[concepts/collective-identity|collective identity]] themes central to the project.

**Link:** [microsolidarity.cc](https://www.microsolidarity.cc/)
