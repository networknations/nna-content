A community currency and mutual credit system operating primarily in Kenya, founded by [[people/will-ruddick|Will Ruddick]] through his organization Grassroots Economics. Sarafu tokens are used by local communities to enable exchange, mutual support, and resource sharing outside formal banking systems. The network represents a practical instantiation of [[concepts/functional-sovereignty|functional sovereignty]] in the economic domain — communities governing their own exchange mechanisms.

Sarafu demonstrates that Web3-adjacent tools (community tokens, distributed ledgers) can enable [[concepts/mutualization|mutualization]] in contexts far removed from the crypto-libertarian mainstream, and that the principles of [[concepts/commons|commons]]-based resource sharing can be implemented at the grassroots level in low-income communities.
