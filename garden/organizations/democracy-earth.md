A non-profit organization co-founded by [[people/santiago-siri|Santiago Siri]] and others, dedicated to building blockchain-based democratic governance infrastructure. Democracy Earth's work includes developing sovereign identity systems, on-chain voting protocols, proof of personhood mechanisms, and the concept of "social smart contracts" — governance agreements encoded as transparent, auditable code.

Democracy Earth represents the technical layer of Network Nations' institutional dimension: building the cryptographic infrastructure that enables [[concepts/self-governance|self-governance]] and [[concepts/functional-sovereignty|functional sovereignty]] for distributed communities without dependence on state-issued identity or centralized institutions. Their work on liquid democracy and delegative voting is directly applicable to the Network Nations governance stack.
