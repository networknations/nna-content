A movement of civic stewards creating the conditions for a participatory, resilient, life-affirming civilization to emerge. OpenCivics convenes practitioners, researchers, and organizations working on civic innovation, governance, and regenerative coordination, treating civic infrastructure as a commons to be co-stewarded across communities.

Several Network Nations Alliance stewards are connected to OpenCivics, including [[people/patricia-parkinson|Patricia Parkinson]] (co-founder) and [[people/stefan-morales|Stefan Morales]] (delegate).
