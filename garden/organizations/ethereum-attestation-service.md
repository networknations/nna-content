An open-source public good for making attestations on or off-chain. EAS provides infrastructure for verifiable claims and credentials — a key building block for [[concepts/stake-based-governance|stake-based governance]] and identity in [[concepts/network-nations|Network Nations]].

Attestation-based systems offer a way for communities to verify membership, contributions, reputation, and governance participation without relying on centralized authorities. See also the [[reading/attestation-based-governance|Attestation Based Governance Systems]] paper.

**Link:** [attest.org](https://attest.org/)
