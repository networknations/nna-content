A European Union-funded research project focused on distributed governance at the local, institutional, and global level. The project is the academic home of the Network Nations podcast series and a key institutional context for [[people/primavera-de-filippi|Primavera De Filippi]] and [[people/felix-beer|Felix Beer]]'s work.

Blockchain.gov frames the Network Nations series as a research-practice feedback loop: bringing together theorists and practitioners to conceptualize [[concepts/network-nations|Network Nations]] and test these ideas against empirical community examples. The project's broader mandate includes blockchain governance, digital commons, and new institutional forms for networked societies.
