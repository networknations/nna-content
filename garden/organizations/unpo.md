An international organization established to facilitate the voices of unrepresented and marginalized nations and peoples worldwide. UNPO provides a platform for those who are not adequately represented in major international institutions such as the United Nations.

UNPO is a key reference point for [[concepts/network-nations|Network Nations]] — it demonstrates that there is already an institutional ecosystem for communities that exercise collective identity and governance outside the traditional state system. The existence of UNPO validates the premise that many peoples lack adequate representation and seek alternative forms of sovereignty.

**Link:** [unpo.org](https://unpo.org/)
